Our Story

Founded by manga lovers, Yume Café is a Japanese-inspired haven located in Fort Bonifacio where you can read, relax, and recharge. Whether you're into shonen, shojo, seinen, or slice-of-life, we’ve got a cozy corner just for you.

  • 📚 5,000+ manga titles in English and Japanese
  • 🍣 Authentic Japanese snacks & drinks
  • 🛋️ Private reading booths and tatami lounges
  • 🎭 Monthly cosplay and manga swap events

Why Yume Café Feels Like Japan

  • 🎐 Tatami-style seating and paper lanterns
  • 📖 Manga in both English and Japanese
  • 🎎 Monthly cosplay and manga swap events
  • 🖌️ Manga drawing workshops and fan art displays
